Brookie Pancakes Recipe

Description

This brookie pancake recipes has a prep time of 20 min and a cooking time of 31 mins, which makes in total a 56 mins recipe, with 6 servings.

Per saving: 749 calories; protein 10.4g; carbohydrates 93g; fat 40g; cholesterol 104.8mg; sodium 747.9mg.

Ingredients

Pancakes

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup semisweet chocolate chips

Brownie Swirl

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package brownie mix (such as Betty Crocker®)
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup water
